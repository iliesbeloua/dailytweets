What is Caffeine?
According to Wikipedia, Caffeine is a central nervous system stimulant of the methylxanthine class.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

It is a chemical found in tea, coffee, carbonated drinks, and some energy drinks.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What Amount of Caffeine is Too Much?
On average Americans drink up to 3 cups of coffee per day. Which contains 100mg of caffeine.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There exists a LETHAL dose of caffeine, which is 10g. But an average cup of coffee contains only 100mg to 200mg of caffeine. It means you need to consume at least 50 to 100 cups of coffee a day to reach the max limit of caffeine which will be lethal for you.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

So, it is almost impossible to overdose caffeine to a lethal level with a normal routine.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

But, there is an overdose that can make you feel no good and can affect our body in negative ways. FDA only recommends 4 to 5 cups of coffee, for a healthy adult, in a day.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Effects of Caffeine on Human Body

Keeps Focused and Energetic
