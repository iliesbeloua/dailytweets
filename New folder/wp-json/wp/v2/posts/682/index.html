1. Why did you start smoking?
First of all, before taking your first step towards quit-smoking therapy, you need to find the reason why you have started smoking and when? And when you find or remember that reason, you need to check if that problem or condition still persists with you or not.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If that problem or condition is still with you then you need to cut it off, try your best to solve your problem or put yourself out from that condition. Try to forget it\u2026 Because if you will still have that problem in your life then you might remember on some point and your left smoking habit might again trigger.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

So this is your first step towards quit-smoking\u2026<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2. Find a reason to Quit Smoking
You must have a strong reason to quit your smoking habit because it will help you stay motivated all the time whenever you will think to smoke again.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The reason might be to save your family or near ones from secondhand smoking (passive smoking) or save yourself from lung cancer, mouth cancer or heart diseases.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you have tried to quit smoking before and failed, don\u2019t let that be an obstacle.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Choose a reason that is strong enough to outweigh the urge to light up\u2026<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3. Tell your near ones
Tell everyone who is near to you and your loved ones that you want to quit smoking and taking measures to quit smoking. Ask for their support, it will help you a lot. Quitting is easier with support.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Ask them to stop you every time whenever you think of smoking or try to light up the cigarette again. They will listen to you and will distract you.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This will help a lot\u2026<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4. Oral supply replacement
Once you are addicted to cigarette, your mouth is also addicted to being busy with it all the time. In easy words, you also attain oral addiction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

So, when you will not smoke for some time, your mouth will crave for something to have in it. You might fill this craving with chewing gum, appetizers, nuts, carrots, raw vegetables, etc. Anything you would like to chew.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5. Remove all reminders
You must keep your environment smoking free, you should remove all smoking remainders you might have in your bedroom, home, office or car. Like, ashtray, cigarette buds, smoke, smoking posters, lighters, and matches, etc.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Cigarette smoke is also a trigger to smoking, so also avoid it as much as possible. This will be of great help\u2026<\/p>\n\n\n\n

6. Quit Slowly don\u2019t panic
As you know, success always comes slowly! So, this sentence applies to everything as long as we are on this planet. You need to remember this every time you leave a cigarette or throw it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Quit smoking one day at a time. It will be easier for you, once you start quitting. Quit one at a time then try to add up days and then follow the same strategy. Don\u2019t try to quit at once, because it could be overwhelming.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Always remember, smoking is never been powerful than you! You can do it!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

7. Maintenance and Stress management
Maintenance:
Because you have been a cigarette addict, that\u2019s why you would have the cravings in intervals. But you must think at that point that how strong you have been in quitting.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Remember your reasons to quit. And don\u2019t let your addiction to overcome you, because it is not stronger than you and will never be.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Start to see yourself as a non-smoker. That is the ultimate payoff. You are freeing yourself from the control of your addiction.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
